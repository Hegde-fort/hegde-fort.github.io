abstract: "Miscalibrated trust — users who over-rely on or inappropriately dismiss AI recommendations — degrades human-AI team performance. We conduct a large-scale online study examining how explanation format (counterfactual vs. feature-importance vs. none) affects trust calibration across demographic groups. We find counterfactual explanations significantly improve calibration for users with lower AI literacy, and propose a model of trust dynamics as a function of explanation quality and past outcome feedback."
