  - title: "Resonance in Weight Space: Covariate Shift Can Drive Divergence of SGD with Momentum"
    description: "We show that SGD with momentum under covariate shift with a fixed step-size can be unstable and diverge. Specifically, SGDm under covariate shift behaves as a parametric oscillator and can suffer from resonance. We characterize this through a time-varying ODE approximation and show empirically that resonance persists under non-periodic covariate shift, neural networks, and optimizers beyond SGDm. Published at ICLR 2022."
